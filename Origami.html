Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Chaeleon

  1. THEIR FEET WORK LIKE SALAD TONGS.
  2. ALMOST HALF OF ALL KNOWN SPECIES LIVE IN MADAGASCAR
  3. THEY MAINLY CHANGE COLOR IN ORDER TO COMMUNICATE OR REGULATE BODY TEMPERATURE.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  1. There are around 3,000 cicada species
  2. They vary in size from 0.75 to 2.25 in (2.2 to 5.5 cm) long.
  3. Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes.

Interesting Facts about teddy

  1. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  2. She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903
  3. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906..
